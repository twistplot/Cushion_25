FAQ

What is Cushion?

Cushion is a Solana-based memecoin engineered to rise when major cryptocurrencies fall. It leverages automated buybacks, staking rewards, and a dynamic treasury to offer stability in volatile markets.

How do buybacks work?

When market dips occur, our system uses treasury funds to buy back Cushion tokens. These tokens are then burned, reducing the circulating supply and supporting the token's value.
